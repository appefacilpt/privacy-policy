Privacy Policy (GDPR)

Last updated: 28th February 2025

Summary:

We respect the EU's Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) and this policy explains how we collect and treat any information you give us. As our users are from Europe, we ensure the following user rights: request access to their data, correct or delete their data, withdraw consent for data storage. The user can request to do it at any time by contacting us at app.efacil.pt@gmail.com

Our policy covers:

  • Why we value your privacy
  • What information we hold & what it is used for
  • Where we store your information & how we do it
  • Data retention
  • Data sharing with third parties
  • Who’s responsible for your information at our company
  • How to complain
  • Changes to the policy

Why we value your privacy

We value your privacy as much as we do our own, so we’re committed to keeping your personal and business information safe. We’re uncomfortable with the information companies, governments, and other organisations keep on file, so we ask for only the bare minimum from our customers. We’ll never use your personal information for any reason other than why you gave it, and we’ll never give anyone access to it unless we’re forced to by law.

What information we hold & what it is used for

  • Email — for creating an account and password recovery.
  • Encrypted password — for secure access to the application.
  • User progress (level, verbs, training history) — for saving and restoring data across different devices.

Where we store your information & how we do it

The data is stored in Google Firebase in compliance with security standards:

  • Email and password are encrypted.
  • User progress is stored in a database with restricted access.

Data retention

The data is stored as long as the user uses the application and is deleted upon request or when the account is deleted.

Data sharing with third parties

We do not share user data with third parties and use it solely for the operation of the application.

Who’s responsible for your information at our company

Darya Verameichyk is responsible for the security of your information. You can contact her by email at app.efacil.pt@gmail.com if you have any concerns about the information we store.

How to complain

We take complaints very seriously. If you’ve any reason to complain about the ways we handle your privacy, please contact Darya Verameichyk by email at app.efacil.pt@gmail.com.

Changes to the policy

If we change the contents of this policy, those changes will become effective the moment we publish them on our GitHub page.
